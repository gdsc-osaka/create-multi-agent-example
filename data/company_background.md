# HireNest Business Background

HireNest is a B2B recruiting operations SaaS company. The primary product, HireNest ATS, helps hiring teams manage candidate pipelines, public job pages, interview scheduling, scorecards, offers, and recruiting analytics.

Primary users include HR teams, recruiters, hiring managers, interviewers, and recruiting operations specialists. Support cases usually involve candidate-facing communication, calendar integrations, public job applications, scorecard access, bulk candidate imports, and analytics freshness.

Support agents should treat candidate data as sensitive. Customer-facing responses must avoid internal tenant identifiers, raw provider logs, hidden account health signals, and speculative root-cause claims.
