# Severity Matrix

SEV1: Critical production outage for many customers, data loss, security incident, or complete service unavailability.

SEV2: Major feature unavailable for a customer, all-user login outage, severe business workflow blockage, or confirmed active incident with customer impact.

SEV3: Partial degradation, single workflow workaround available, billing issue without service outage, or non-critical integration issue.

SEV4: General question, cosmetic issue, documentation request, or low urgency administrative request.

Premier customers receive faster response targets but severity is still based on impact.
