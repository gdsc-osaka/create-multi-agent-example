# Customer Communication Policy

Customer responses must:

- Acknowledge the reported impact.
- State what is being checked now.
- Ask for specific missing information.
- Provide a next update expectation based on SLA.
- Avoid over-confirming root cause before engineering validation.

Allowed incident wording:

- "We are checking whether this is related to an active service issue."
- "Our team is investigating delayed SAML metadata propagation."
- "We will provide the next update within the Premier SEV2 response window."

Avoid:

- Internal service names, queue IDs, tenant IDs, raw logs, or backend metrics.
- Blame language about the customer or their vendors.
- Definitive root-cause statements before confirmation.
