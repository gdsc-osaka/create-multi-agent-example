# Billing and Invoices FAQ

Monthly invoices can differ from the base contract value when seat counts change during the billing period.

For flexible seat true-up customers, added seats are prorated for the remainder of the billing period and appear as separate line items. Finance admins can request a seat-change audit export.

Customer-safe guidance:

- Confirm the invoice number and billing period.
- Confirm the date and number of seats added.
- Explain prorated true-up charges without exposing internal billing ledger IDs.
