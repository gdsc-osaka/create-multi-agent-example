# Incident Communication Runbook

Customer-facing incident updates should be factual, time-bound, and avoid root-cause certainty until engineering confirms it.

Allowed:

- Acknowledge the reported impact.
- State that the team is investigating a possible relation to an active incident.
- Provide next update timing according to SLA.

Avoid:

- Internal incident timelines.
- Raw metrics, backend component names, or unconfirmed root cause.
- Blaming the customer or their vendors.
